A Cherished Canvas: The Portrait of a Radiant Soul.

Being an artist, my eyes were struck,
On a gem of a person-and I decided to paint,
My heart decided to fill the canvas with the richness of red,
To paint the picture of a passionate lover.

Eyes brewing with the desire for union with a beloved,
And heart craving to entitle his beloved,
An enigmatic soul desires to be a knight,
To liberate a damsel in distress.

I decided to embrace the portrait with blueness,
Mistakenly, a blue stroke overlaps a red stroke,
Which manifests the shadow of an inner temptest,
Diligently, I persist to paint the perfect 'one'.

Indigo swirls delineate the Ajna Chakra,
It emblemizes a yearning for enlightenment,
Ocean-deep eyes assures stability,
And a heart veiled by a wisp of innocence.

Blended red and green to encompass groundedness,
The power of transformation is expressed through light brown,
A confident smile represents radiant assurance,
And a seren face blossoms the sense of security.

Used white color to finalize the portrait,
The whiteness of the swan painted—the tranquil expression,
Mesmerized by the portrait,
I fell in love with my creation.
