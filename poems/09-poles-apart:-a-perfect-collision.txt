Poles Apart: A Perfect Collision

Your fierce soul brightens my world,
Your soulful eyes assures me of stability and security.

Innocence reflecting from your eyes,
Allows my heart to uncage my inner child.

Your intense love and care for your closed ones,
Increases my longing to be in that close circle.

Your stern interest to decipher my personality,
Helps me to regain my self worth.

Your strong yearning for my undivided attention,
Makes me feel needed in your fast moving world.

Whispers of your unsaid words states that,
Hold on we can get through this,

Being a polar apart,
We're trying to attain perfect collision.
