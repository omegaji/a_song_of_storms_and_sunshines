A Profound Embrace Meant For Special One

I feel like I'm starving all day,
Nothing even feels to fill this pit.
Deepens in every possible way,
A hug would fill it only half,
While an embrace will do the trick.

I'll tuck you in and wrap you up,
With my arms that forever long,
Drop my cheek on yours,
As I tighten my hold into you.

And I tighten it and tighten it even more,
Until I stamp my soul and body on you,
Which last forever and ever,
And speaks to the whole world.

Of whom this bearer... belongs too,
Of whom this bearer...is cherished by,
Of whom this bearer...loves,
Of whom this bearer...is loved by.
