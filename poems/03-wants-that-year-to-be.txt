Wants That Year To Be

I want to wrap myself in your arms to feel your presence
I want to lean my forehead against yours to feel the calmness
I want to close my eyes and see the dreams through your gaze
I want my dry wood heart to ignite with your blazing fire
I want to hear my inner voice reflected in your words
I want to inhale earthiness through your breath
I want to paint with your colors
I want to write with your imagination
