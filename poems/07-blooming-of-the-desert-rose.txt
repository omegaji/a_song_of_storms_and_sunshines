Blooming of the Desert Rose

Soul of a deserted island,
Yearns for explorer's footprint,
Aspiring for the dampness,
To quench the thirst.
Barrens the dreamy world,
Which makes life a painful sleep,
Tranquility wrapped by seclusion,
Weighed down by unexpressed tenderness.
Holding on for the arrival of an explorer,
To enlighten with sunshine,
And embark on serenity through companionship.
