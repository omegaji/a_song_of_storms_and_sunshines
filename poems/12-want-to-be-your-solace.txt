Want To Be Your Solace

In times of distress and unyeilding stress,
I want to hold you tight with a strong press.

Even if you tug, shrug, and say no,
I'll still hold you with no intension of letting go.

Cause I want to let you loose back into the world,
Only when you've been born new from my hold.

Energized, cheery with a jolly look on the face,
You'll be back stronger than ever in life's race.
