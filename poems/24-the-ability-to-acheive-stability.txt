The ability to achieve stability

In this ever-evolving world,
Yearning for stability is what everyone wants,
The term is largely misbelieved as a physical state of being,
This misconception leads to serious repercussions.
Humans desire a state that can't be attained,
As the term "stability" refers to a mental state,
Deep yearning for a physical state of stability,
Leads humans to the stillness of the lake,
A tight hold of stillness over a period of time can suffocate,
Term instability is widely perceived as rootlessness,
The state that humans fear to be in,
This fear makes them cling onto a misperceived notion of stability,
Yearning for stability leads to impending growth,
Yearning for stability leads to cuddling ignorance tightly,
Yearning for stability chains the possibility of blooming,
Yearning for stability leads to deep devotion for a well-trodden road,
Yearning for stability hinders the development of resilience,
Yearning for stability diminishes the power of adaptability,
Yearning for stability leads to a state of inner turmoil,
The ever-moving ocean reminds us stability is a state of mind,
A determined soul can attain it at any stage of life.
