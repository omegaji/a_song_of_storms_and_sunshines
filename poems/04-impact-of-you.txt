Impact of You

In this freezing cold,
You light up my soul.
In this creepy tranquillity,
Your words restore zest.

While wandering on barren stretch of distance,
Your endearing smile rejuvenates my world.

Outpaced by the world,
Your companionship restores my self-worth.

When my rigidity breaks me apart,
Your fluidity lets me flow.

When my countless fears hold me back,
Your fearless wings let me fly.
