Rejuvenated by a Kind Soul

My mind is a strom,
Feeling all kinds of things,
Where are you from?
Bringer of calm to this unstable soul.

Your words are like wisdom,
A song calming my noise,
Staggering off the path some,
Luckily I have you who bring my poise.

I want to welcom you,
A gem among the rarest few,
With messy poetry I try to say,
I love you and will always do.
